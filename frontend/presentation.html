Today's AI landscape is dominated by isolated assistants and raw APIs. They're powerful, but they don't mirror how real work gets done. Real work happens in teams: planners, builders, analysts, designers, marketers — all coordinating around a mission.

Crew-7 turns that reality into a product. Each Crew is a 7-agent AI team: one Orchestrator and six specialists who communicate using a hybrid model — top-down planning plus peer-to-peer collaboration. Users describe a mission, and a Crew executes it end-to-end, producing real architectures, code, documents, strategies, or campaigns.

We provide three pillars: a secure execution engine for multi-agent runs, a real-time Agent Graph to visualize and trust what's happening, and a marketplace where crews can be discovered, installed, rented, or owned as digital assets.

Our MVP focuses on software and product-centric crews, because dev and startup ecosystems move fastest and give us the clearest feedback loops. Over time, we expand into marketing, finance, operations, and research.

We're raising a pre-seed round to build the first full version of Crew-7: a platform where AI teams are as real, trusted, and productive as human teams — and sometimes faster.

Product Roadmap

0–6 Months — MVP & Early Crews:

  • Ship Crew-7 MVP: FastAPI backend, Crew engine, runs, traces, sandbox.
  • Implement Chat + Advanced Mode (Agent Graph) with WebSocket events.
  • Launch blueprint-style landing page with anime.js storytelling.
  • Offer initial crews: Backend Builder, Frontend Builder, SaaS Starter, Pitch Deck Crew.
  • Run closed beta with devs and small teams; collect qualitative feedback.

6–9 Months — Marketplace & Multi-Domain Expansion:

  • Open Marketplace with installable crew templates.
  • Allow creators to publish and manage their own crews.
  • Add more domain crews: Marketing, Research, Business/Finance.
  • Improve observability: richer traces, filters, mission lab features.
  • Begin revenue experiments: subscriptions + basic marketplace economics.

9–12 Months — Scaling & Web3-Ready Layer:

  • Optimize performance and reliability for higher run volume.
  • Introduce optional Web3 metadata for crews (reputation, ownership, royalties).
  • Pilot with select agencies and AI-native teams for workflow integration.
  • Refine UX for crew creation/editing; improve Agent Graph editing for power users.
  • Prepare for seed round with usage metrics, marketplace traction, and real case studies.
